Purpose: Monitors Wise transfers and automatically logs them to Airtable for record-keeping and accounting.

What it does:
Trigger & Monitoring:
- Listens for transfer state changes via Wise webhook
- Monitors a specific Wise profile 
- Automatically triggers when transfers are created, updated, or completed

Data Processing:
- Fetches detailed transfer information using the transfer ID

Extracts key data points:
- Transfer ID
- Creation date
- Reference/description
- Amount transferred

Record Keeping:
- Stores all transfer data in Airtable 
- Creates an audit trail of all money movements
- Maintains organized financial records

Use Case:
This is perfect for:
- Accounting and bookkeeping automation
- Financial reporting and audit trails
- Expense tracking across multiple transfers
- Business finance management
