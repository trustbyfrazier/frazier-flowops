Purpose: Automatically announces new WooCommerce products on both Twitter and Telegram.

What it does:
- Trigger: Listens for new product creation in WooCommerce
- Multi-platform Publishing: Simultaneously posts to:
- Twitter (public announcement)
- Telegram (channel/group announcement)
- Content: Product announcement format:
"✨ New Product Announcement ✨"
Product name
Direct link to product page

Use Case:
Perfect for e-commerce stores using WooCommerce that want to automatically promote new products across multiple social platforms, reaching both Twitter followers and Telegram community members simultaneously.
