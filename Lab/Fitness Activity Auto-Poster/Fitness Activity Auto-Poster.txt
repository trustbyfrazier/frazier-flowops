Purpose: Automatically shares new Strava workout activities on Twitter.

What it does:
- Trigger: Listens for new activity creation in Strava via webhook
- Content: Automatically tweets workout details including:
- Distance run (in meters)
- Activity name/title
Format: "I ran [distance] meters and completed my [activity name]!"

Use Case:
Perfect for fitness enthusiasts who want to automatically share their workout achievements on social media without manual posting.
