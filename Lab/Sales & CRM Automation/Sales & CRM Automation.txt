Purpose: Syncs Shopify orders to HubSpot CRM as deals and contacts.

What it does:
- Triggers on order updates
- Creates/updates HubSpot contacts with customer data
- Creates deals for orders that don't exist in HubSpot
- Maps order data to CRM fields:
Deal amount, name, close date
Customer contact information
Prevents duplicate deals

Use Case: 
Sales pipeline automation and CRM integration.
