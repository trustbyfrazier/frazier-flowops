Purpose: Monitors Twitter for brand mentions and alerts teams in Slack.

What it does:
- Schedule: Runs every 10 minutes via cron trigger
- Monitoring: Searches for mentions of "@n8n_io" (configurable)
- Smart Filtering: Only processes tweets created since the last run (10-minute window)
- Data Processing: Extracts tweet content and constructs tweet URLs
- Notifications: Posts new mentions to a Slack channel (#recent-tweets) with:
- Tweet text
Direct link to the tweet

Key Features:
- Duplicate prevention using time-based filtering
- Configurable search terms and Slack channels
- Regular monitoring without missing mentions

Use Case:
Ideal for social media teams, customer support, or brand managers who need real-time alerts when their company is mentioned on Twitter.
