Purpose: Sends daily language learning vocabulary via SMS.

What it does:
- Runs daily at 8:00 AM via cron trigger
- Fetches top 3 articles from Hacker News
- Extracts unique words from article titles
- Translates English words to German using LingvaNex
- Saves vocabulary to Airtable for record-keeping
- Sends SMS with 5 daily words (English:German) via Vonage
- Creates a personalized language learning routine
